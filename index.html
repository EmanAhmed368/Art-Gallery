Did You Know?

Fascinating Art Facts

01

The Mona Lisa Has No Eyebrows

It was the fashion in Renaissance Florence to shave them off. Leonardo da Vinci's masterpiece reflects the beauty standards of 16th-century Italy.

02

The Color Wheel Was Invented by Newton

In 1666, Sir Isaac Newton used a prism to split white light into a spectrum, then arranged the colors in a circle—creating the first color wheel.

03

Picasso Could Draw Before He Could Walk

His first word was "piz," short for lápiz—the Spanish word for pencil. He completed his first painting at age nine.
